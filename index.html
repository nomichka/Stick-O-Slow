Welcome to the Game of Sticks!

In this game, you are an All-Powerful Being that has decided to create a More Efficient Universe. To accomplish this task, you'll have to solve problems and stuff using the Power of Programming.

It'll be difficult at times, but I promise to keep my faith in you and help in any way I can. As you play this game, my hope is that you learn advanced programming concepts - so that you can implement them to advance in the game, and so that you can implement them to advance in life.
